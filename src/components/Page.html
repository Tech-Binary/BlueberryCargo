What is an Insurance Surety Bond?

An Insurance Surety Bond is a three-party agreement where an insurer guarantees the obligations of the contractor to the project owner — replacing the need for cash margins or bank guarantees.

Who can apply for a surety bond?

Contractors, EPC firms, infrastructure businesses, and any organisation participating in government or PSU tenders can apply through ProBond.

How long does the issuance process take?

Most surety bonds are issued within 3 to 7 working days, depending on documentation and underwriting requirements.

Are surety bonds accepted by government bodies?

Yes. Surety bonds issued by IRDAI-approved insurers are fully accepted by central and state government bodies, PSUs, and major procurement portals.
